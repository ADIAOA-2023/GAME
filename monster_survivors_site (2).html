About the Game

Monster Survivors is a skill‑based survival arena game inspired by modern action roguelikes. Players are dropped into a monster‑infested battlefield where they must dodge, fight, and upgrade strategically to survive as long as possible.

As waves grow stronger, you can unlock new abilities, enhance your weapons, and discover powerful synergies to maximize your survival time. Whether on PC or mobile, Monster Survivors runs smoothly thanks to responsive design and optimized gameplay.
